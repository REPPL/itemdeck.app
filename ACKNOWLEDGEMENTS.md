## Placeholder Images

### Picsum Photos

**URL:** https://picsum.photos

Development and testing use placeholder images from [Picsum Photos](https://picsum.photos), a free image placeholder service.

**Usage in this project:**
- `src/data/cards.mock.ts` - Mock card data for development
- `src/hooks/useCardData.ts` - Fallback images for items without imageUrl
